Frequently Asked Questions

What is Online Sphere Commerce?
Online Sphere Commerce is a premier digital marketplace and financial management platform. We provide the tools for individuals and teams to engage in secure digital asset trading and network-based earnings growth.
How secure are my fund transfers?
Every transaction on our platform is encrypted using SSL technology and backed by a multi-layer verification process. We ensure that internal transfers and external withdrawals are monitored 24/7 by our security team.
What is the withdrawal limit for unverified accounts?
Unverified accounts are restricted to a maximum withdrawal of $50 per day. To increase your limits and access premium features, please visit the 'Verification' tab and upload your government-issued ID.
How do I contact support for urgent issues?
You can contact our support team directly via the 'Live Chat' feature in the footer or by clicking the WhatsApp support button on your dashboard. Priority support is given to Verified Members.
What are the supported payout methods?
We currently support M-Pesa, Airtel Money, Bank Wire (SWIFT/SEPA), and Cryptocurrency (USDT TRC20). Payouts are typically processed within a 2-hour window.
